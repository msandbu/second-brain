I spend most of my time on agentic AI systems, MCP infrastructure, AI security, hybrid cloud strategy, and how organisations actually succeed with large-scale technology transformation.

---

## Background

20 years in enterprise technology. I have watched every major platform shift from the practitioner side: virtualisation, cloud migration, mobile and BYOD, SaaS consolidation, and now AI. Each one followed a recognisable pattern: vendor hype, enterprise resistance, workers adopting consumer versions anyway, IT eventually providing a governed version. AI is following the same pattern, faster.

I started writing about technology in 2012 on [msandbu.org](https://msandbu.org), which I call "Tech Ramblings." Over 20 years I have published more than 1000 articles, 5 books, and spoken at 200+ public sessions at conferences across Europe. None of it is AI-generated.

My focus has always been on practical outcomes from real deployments. I work with actual enterprise customers, see what works and what does not, and write about that. Not about what vendors say should work.
